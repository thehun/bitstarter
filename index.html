Have you been telling yourself that you would start doing sport (like cycling) but never actually get around to it? Is it because you don't have anyone to go out with? Or maybe because of the weather? Are you tired of the same routes and don't feel like driving hundreds of miles away to explore new trails? You won't have these excuses any more! We are going to develop the first Online Cycling Community, where you will be able to cycle around the world (either just relaxing and enjoying the scenery or actually competing with other cyclists) without having to leave your house.

The goal of the Online Cycling Project is to create a completely interactive social cycling experience. For this purpose, an intelligent static bicycle will be developed, which will be connected to a PC, XBOX or PS3 that will immerse the rider into a virtual world loaded with cycling tracks from all around the world. The bicycle will have an incorporated embedded system that will adjust the difficulty according to the terrain and will provide feedback of the user's input to the central unit. It will be possible to cycle for leisure on all the tracks and also to participate in online cycling competitions with other online cyclists from all over the world, creating an online community where participants can exchange their impressions and perhaps even meet up for cycle tours in the real world.

FAQ

Who wil be the target users of the Online Cycling Project?
The Online Cycling Project will be apt for anyone with a passion for cycling between the ages of 9 to 99. The different levels of difficulty will make it a perfect choice for leisure users as well as for competitive and fit cyclists.
Will I be able to monitor my heartbeat, body temperature, burned calories, etc?
Yes! The bicycle will be equipped with several sensors which will help you monitor these and other variables in order to make this experience not just fun, but also healthy!
